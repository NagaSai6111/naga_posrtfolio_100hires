# Post 1 – SEO Gets Introduces a Free Tier

**Approx. Date:** Recent

# Post 3 – How Users Interact with Modern Google Results

**Approx. Date:** Recent

## Summary

Aleyda discusses eye-tracking research showing that many Google SERP features attract user attention but generate relatively few clicks. AI Overviews, product listings, images, and other rich results often receive visual attention without converting into website visits. Traditional organic listings continue to drive the highest engagement and click-through rates.

## Key Insights

- Attention does not always lead to clicks.
- Organic listings remain valuable.
- AI Overviews change user behaviour.
- Visibility and engagement are different metrics.

## Why It Matters for AI SEO

Businesses should measure both visibility and traffic because AI Search introduces new ways for brands to be discovered without direct website visits.

# Post 4 – Measuring AI Search Share of Voice

**Approx. Date:** Recent

## Summary

Aleyda introduces FreeSOV, a tool designed to measure AI Search visibility. She explains metrics such as AI visibility score, citations, mentions, query fan-out, sentiment analysis, and per-source attribution, helping businesses understand how often and how positively they appear across AI-generated answers.

## Key Insights

- AI visibility requires new KPIs.
- Citation tracking is becoming essential.
- Sentiment analysis improves brand monitoring.
- AI Search performance extends beyond rankings.

## Why It Matters for AI SEO

As AI assistants become discovery platforms, marketers need dedicated measurement frameworks instead of relying solely on traditional ranking reports.
