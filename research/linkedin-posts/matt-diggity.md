# Post 3 – The 5-Step AI Search Playbook

**Approx. Date:** Recent

## Summary

Matt shared a framework explaining how his team improved visibility in AI search. The playbook emphasizes maintaining consistent messaging, aligning content with user intent, strengthening E-E-A-T signals, improving Digital PR, and reducing keyword cannibalization. He argues that AI systems reward brands with authority, clear topical relevance, and strong editorial trust.

## Key Insights

- Maintain consistent messaging across all pages.
- Improve intent alignment before creating more content.
- Strengthen E-E-A-T through expertise and authority.
- Build Digital PR to increase AI citations.
- Remove keyword cannibalization.

## Why It Matters for AI SEO

AI search engines increasingly reference authoritative brands rather than simply ranking pages with strong keyword optimization.

# Post 5 – Seasonal SEO Framework

**Approx. Date:** Recent

## Summary

Matt shares a seasonal SEO strategy that helped one business significantly increase organic sessions and revenue. Rather than waiting until peak season, the team optimized landing pages, refreshed product content, improved Google Merchant Center listings, and built topical clusters months before demand increased.

## Key Insights

- SEO planning should begin months before seasonal demand.
- Refresh existing pages before creating new ones.
- Build topical authority through related content clusters.
- Optimize Merchant Center and structured product data.

## Why It Matters for AI SEO

AI-powered search rewards websites with well-organized content ecosystems and complete topical coverage rather than isolated pages.
